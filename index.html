Biography

I'm an assistant professor at Paris-South University in Orsay. I'm currently working on neutrino physics at the Linear Accelerator Laboratory, looking for Majorana neutrino with the SuperNEMO experiment. Take a look at my C.V. or scroll down to read about some of the projects that I'm working on.
